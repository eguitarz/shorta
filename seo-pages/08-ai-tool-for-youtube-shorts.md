## 3. Search Intent Analysis

### What Problem the User Is Experiencing
Creators are curious whether AI can help them make better Shorts. They've seen AI tools for writing, thumbnails, and video editing, and they're wondering if there's something similar that can analyze and improve their short-form video content. They want to leverage AI, but aren't sure which AI tools are actually useful for Shorts vs. just hype.

### Why Existing Advice or Tools Aren't Helping
- **Content AI (ChatGPT, Jasper):** Writes scripts, but can't analyze finished video content
- **Editing AI:** Automates cuts but doesn't provide creative feedback
- **SEO tools (VidIQ, TubeBuddy):** Focus on titles, tags, thumbnails—not the video itself
- **Thumbnail AI:** Doesn't help with what happens *after* the click
- **Trend analysis tools:** Tell you what's popular, not what's wrong with your video

Creators need AI that watches their actual Short and tells them what to fix.

## 5. Product-Led Explanation

### How Shorta Works for This Use Case:

1. **Upload Your Short**  
   Drag and drop your MP4. Shorta accepts any Short under 60 seconds—published or still in draft form.

2. **AI-Powered Video Analysis**  
   Shorta's AI doesn't just transcribe or add effects. It *watches* your video like an experienced producer and identifies:
   - **Hook effectiveness:** Does the first 2 seconds grab attention?
   - **Pacing analysis:** Are there slow or confusing moments?
   - **Clarity check:** Will first-time viewers understand immediately?
   - **Retention prediction:** Where are viewers most likely to swipe?

3. **Concrete, Actionable Feedback**  
   You'll receive feedback like:
   > "0:00–0:02: Hook buries the interesting element. Front-load the tension ('I almost quit') before the context."
   > "0:06–0:09: Pacing slows. Consider cutting the pause or adding a visual pattern interrupt."

4. **Storyboard Suggestions for Re-Filming**  
   Shorta generates a practical re-filming plan:
   - Reordered scenes for better flow
   - Suggested hook rewrites
   - Flagged sections to cut or re-shoot
