## 3. Search Intent Analysis

### What Problem the User Is Experiencing
Creators are making Shorts in a vacuum. They film, edit, and post without any outside feedback—then wonder why views are low. They want honest, expert-level feedback before publishing, but don't have access to producers, editors, or knowledgeable friends who can review their work.

### Why Existing Advice or Tools Aren't Helping
- **No feedback loop:** Most creators post → flop → guess → repeat
- **Friends and family are too nice:** "Looks great!" doesn't catch the hook that buries the lead
- **Hiring a consultant is expensive:** And slow—you can't get feedback on every Short
- **YouTube Studio is post-mortem:** It only tells you something was wrong *after* the damage is done
- **Self-review is biased:** You can't see your video with fresh eyes

Creators need fast, honest, actionable feedback before they hit publish.

## 5. Product-Led Explanation

### How Shorta Works for This Use Case:

1. **Upload Your Short**  
   Before you post to YouTube, drop your MP4 into Shorta. Think of it as getting your rough cut reviewed by a producer.

2. **Automatic Feedback Generation**  
   Shorta's AI watches your video like an experienced producer and identifies:
   - **Hook issues:** Is the first 2 seconds doing its job?
   - **Pacing problems:** Slow spots, dead air, rushed transitions
   - **Clarity gaps:** Moments where viewers might get confused
   - **Retention risks:** Sections where swiping is likely

3. **Concrete, Actionable Feedback**  
   Instead of vague notes, you get specific ones:
   > "0:00–0:02: Hook relies on context that viewers don't have. Front-load the outcome."
   > "0:09–0:12: Pacing lags here. Consider cutting or adding a pattern interrupt."

4. **Storyboard Suggestions for Re-Filming**  
   Shorta generates a revised storyboard:
   - Recommends scene order changes
   - Flags sections to cut or re-shoot
   - Gives you a clear re-filming plan
