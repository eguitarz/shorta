# Shorta x AppSumo — Talking Points

## The Pitch (30 seconds)

Shorta is an AI video coach for YouTube creators. Paste a video URL or upload a draft, get beat-by-beat analysis of what's killing retention, then generate a filmable storyboard with director's notes to fix it. Think of it as having a creative director who watches every trending Short and tells you exactly what to change.

## The Problem

- 500+ hours of video uploaded to YouTube every minute
- Creators guess what works — no data-driven feedback loop before publishing
- Existing tools generate scripts; none analyze *why viewers leave*
- YouTube Shorts is exploding but creators have zero analytics on draft content

## The Product

| Feature | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| Video Analyzer | Beat-by-beat AI analysis: hook score, retention timeline, pacing, clarity |
| Storyboard Generator | Filmable storyboards with camera angles, energy cues, director's notes |
| Long-Form Support | Chapter-level analysis for tutorials, vlogs, etc. |
| Free SEO Tools | 8 specialized tools (niche analyzer, hook analyzer, etc.) driving organic traffic |

- Works with YouTube URLs, uploads, and unpublished drafts
- Powered by Google Gemini AI
- Credit-based usage model (already built and live)

## Target Audience

- YouTube Shorts creators (casual to daily)
- UGC creators making ads for brands/agencies
- Content studios managing multiple channels
- Startups iterating on video ad creatives

## Why AppSumo Makes Sense Now

- Product is live, polished, and functional
- Credit system is already built — perfect for tiered AppSumo codes
- Need user acquisition and social proof for organic growth
- YouTube creator market is massive and active on AppSumo

## Handle Objections

**"Why should we feature a pre-revenue product?"**
> Many top AppSumo launches are from early-stage products. We're using this as our go-to-market strategy, which means we'll be highly motivated to deliver a great experience and earn strong reviews.

**"What about AI costs on a lifetime deal?"**
> Our credit-based system caps usage per month. We're not offering unlimited — each tier has defined monthly credits. This keeps unit economics sustainable long-term.

**"What makes you different from ChatGPT?"**
> ChatGPT generates scripts from a blank page. Shorta analyzes existing videos to find what's actually working, then generates storyboards based on data — not generic viral patterns. It's a feedback loop, not a one-shot generator.
